About

Leandro Lozada is a photojournalist based in Louisville, KY. He uses his camera and drone to document relevant stories.

His clients include the Washington Post, AFP, Politico Magazine, and Al Día News. His work has appeared in The Guardian, NPR, La Jornada, El País, The New York Times, CNN, and other publications.

He is available for assignments around the country and internationally.

Besides photography, Leandro loves his family, coffee, motorcycles, movies, music, books, and soccer.
